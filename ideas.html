Delta Green Character Ideas & Inspiration

Stuck on a concept? Use these prompts for Delta Green agents and NPCs, then open the character creator to turn an idea into a full fillable sheet.

Profession prompts

Federal agent under pressure

A field office investigator who has seen too much red tape—and one case that should not exist.

Scientist who saw too much

A lab researcher whose peer-reviewed world collapsed after one sample that moved wrong.

Local law with old secrets

A sheriff deputy in a county where missing persons reports get filed and forgotten.

Civilian expert on retainer

A consultant Green Box brought in twice—now they cannot unknow the pattern.

Bond & motivation hooks

  • Bond: A sibling who thinks you work fraud investigations—keep them safe by lying.
  • Bond: A partner who survived the last op; they do not remember what you did to save them.
  • Motivation: Prove the last report was real, even if it costs your career.
  • Motivation: Retire with one normal year—before the next summons.

Community character library
