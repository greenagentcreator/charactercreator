How to Create a Delta Green Character

This guide explains Delta Green RPG character creation from first concept to a playable agent dossier—the same workflow as our free online character creator and fillable character sheet.

Character creation steps

  1. Choose profession and skills Select a standard or custom profession and allocate skill points.
  2. Define statistics Assign or roll STR, CON, DEX, INT, POW, and CHA.
  3. Calculate derived attributes Confirm HP, WP, SAN, and other derived values from your stats.
  4. Set bonds and motivations Describe personal connections and what drives your agent.
  5. Add personal details Enter name, appearance, background, and equipment.
  6. Save, print, or share Review the summary, save locally, print the sheet, or share a link.

Tips for a strong agent

  • Choose a profession that fits the operation—combat skills are not always the answer in Delta Green.
  • Bonds and motivations drive roleplay; write them as story hooks, not just numbers.
  • Leave room on the sheet for notes, gear, and Adaptations after the scenario starts.
