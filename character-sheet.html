Delta Green Online & Fillable Character Sheet

A browser-based fillable character sheet for Delta Green agents—update skills and stats during play, roll from the sheet, and print a clean dossier when you need paper at the table.

What you can do on the sheet

  • Edit the full agent dossier after creation—skills, stats, bonds, gear, and notes
  • Click skills to roll directly in the browser (no separate dice app required)
  • Auto-save changes in your browser; export or import JSON backups
  • Print-friendly layout for handouts and table play
  • Share a link so your Handler or group can import the same agent

Online sheet vs. PDF fillables

PDF fillables are fine for one-shot printing, but they are awkward mid-session. This online sheet stays editable, handles rolls, and pairs with the built-in character creator so you do not re-type data.
