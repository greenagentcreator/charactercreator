Frequently asked questions

How do I make a Delta Green character?

Use this free character creator: pick a profession and skills, roll or assign statistics, set bonds and motivations, add personal details, then save or print your agent dossier. Click Start creating your agent to begin.

Is this a fillable online character sheet?

Yes. After creation you get an editable online sheet with skill rolls, notes, and items. You can update it anytime, export JSON, or print a clean layout for play.

Do I need an account or install anything?

No. The tool runs in your browser. Characters save on your device unless you optionally share to the community library.

Can I use this as a character generator or builder?

Yes—it works as a Delta Green character generator and builder with official-style professions, custom professions, and a guided wizard for new agents.

Where can I find Delta Green character ideas?

Scroll to the community library on the home screen to browse shared agents, or start the wizard and use bonds, motivations, and professions as hooks for your own concept.
